Coast Diagnostics in the News: COVID Antibody Testing

January 5th, 2021

Fox10 News Reporting:

MOBILE, Ala. --COVID Antibody Testing is now available at Coast Diagnostics at 4674 Airport Boulevard, Suite C in Mobile, Alabama.
